LAGOS STATE DIGITAL SUMMIT 2022

The goal of the Lagos Digital Summit 2022 is to bring together all tech enthusiasts, including business owners, freelancers, start-up founders, MDAs, FinTechs, students from inside and beyond the state, corporate bodies, and the general public. This one-day summit will discuss and promote digital technologies and skills for the future, as well as how these abilities might be applied in the near future to solve pressing real-world issues.
